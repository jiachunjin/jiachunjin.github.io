About Me

Biography

I am an undergraduate with the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, China. I was born in October, 1997, in Shanghai, China. My research interests cover machine learning and pattern recognition, especially the non-deep-learning topics with good interpretability. I was once a football player certificated as national second-level athlete when I was a high school student, and now I am interested in analyzing professional football games and developing strategies for them with artificial intelligence.
